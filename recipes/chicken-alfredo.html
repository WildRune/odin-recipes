Chicken Alfredo

A classic Italian dish that can be enjoyed on any occasion.

This chicken alferdo recipe can help in a pinch when you can't quite decide what you'd like to do for dinner while still delivering a tasty and nutritious meal.

It's especially awesome when it's cold outside!

Ingredients:

  • Cooking cil of your choice
  • 4 (4 ounce) skinless boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 (0.7 ounce) packet of Italian-style salad dressing mix
  • 1 clove of garlic, pressed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can Condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Follow Along:

  1. Cover the crock of a slow cooker with your cooking oil.
  2. Place your chicken breasts into the crock.
  3. Combine the water and Italian dressing mix in a small bowl.
  4. Pour over chicken, sprinkle with garlic, cover, and cook on low for 4 hours.
  5. After 4 hours, whisk cream cheese and condensed soup together in a bowl.
  6. Pour over chicken and cook on low for 1 hour.
  7. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to boil over high heat.
  8. Stir in spaghetti and return to a boil.
  9. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until al dente (roughly 12 minutes). Then drain well.
  10. To serve, spoon chicken and sauce over hot cooked pasta and sprinkle with parsley.
