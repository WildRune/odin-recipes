A perfect meal to end a day of hardwork.

This ribeye recipe is the only thing you need to recover your muscles and relax after a long day's work.

It's especially versatile and may be served with many side dishes!

Ingredients:

  • Cast iron skillet
  • Cut of ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Crushed garlic
  • Rosemary

Follow Along:

  1. Preheat your cast iron skillet over the stove on medium heat.
  2. Place a small portion of butter in the pan.
  3. Cook the ribeye for about 8 minutes flipping frequently (about every minute).
  4. Turn the heat up to high and add in more butter.
  5. Add in your crushed garlic and rosemary and spoon the butter over the steak constantly for 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes.
